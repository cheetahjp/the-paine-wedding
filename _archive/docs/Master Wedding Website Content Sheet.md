We avoided each other because we didn’t know if the other one would want to talk. Jeffrey texted Ashlyn after the game just saying that he hopes I am doing well. Jeffrey asked one more time to hang out and Ashlyn agreed. Jeffrey drove 4.5 hours to Houston to take Ashlyn on a date at Galveston Bay Brewing. We talked for hours and it felt like no time had passed, but we were both different people and had experienced a lot of life in the years we spent apart. 2 months later Ashlyn and Jeffrey started dating again on October 18th, 2024\. We have spent the year and a half dating long distance\! Visiting every other week, lots of long car rides, facetime calls, and really hard goodbyes after fun weekends\! We can’t wait to spend the rest of our lives together.

Jeffrey got down on one knee and asked Ashlyn to spend forever with him and she said, “YES, YES, YES, YES I WILL\!” The evening was still full of surprises when Ashlyn and Jeffrey spent a fun private dinner together at 60 Vines and talked about all the planning that went into the engagement.
